Description

This prefab includes a 2d alien boss that can be used in your game.

The boss has an internal state machine which allows it to move between the following states:

Idle: the boss will remain stationary with an idle animation.

Take Damage: the boss will decrease it's internal health by 10 and play a damage animation, flashing red.

Die: when the boss's health reaches 0, it will die and play a death animation. Once the animation has finished, it will remove itself from the scene.

Attack: The attack state will trigger the boss to play a particular animation, and launch 4 fireballs across the screen to cause damage to the player.

This will allow you instantly add a boss to your 2d game. The included C# code is simple and easy to use. It is also highly modular, allowing you to customize the code to your exact needs easily.

Requirements

Unity 2022.3.43f or later
